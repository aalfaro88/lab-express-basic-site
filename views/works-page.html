About the Works

Some of Van Gogh's most famous works include:

  • "The Starry Night" (1889):

    • This iconic painting depicts a nighttime sky with swirling stars and a crescent moon. It is known for its expressive brushwork and vivid color palette.

  • "Sunflowers" series (1888-1889):

    • Van Gogh painted multiple versions of sunflowers, exploring different arrangements and color schemes. These paintings are characterized by their vibrant yellow hues and textured brushstrokes.

  • "Irises" (1889):

    • This floral still-life painting showcases a bouquet of irises in various shades of blue and purple. Van Gogh created this artwork during his time in a psychiatric hospital.

  • "Café Terrace at Night" (1888):

    • This painting depicts a café terrace in Arles, France, with vibrant colors and a sense of nighttime ambiance. It is known for its unique perspective and the contrast between warm and cool tones.
